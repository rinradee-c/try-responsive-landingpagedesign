New map

The Chasm

The Chasm is an area located west of Lisha, Liyue, and is the primary source of ores in the nation. Recently, a series of mysterious accidents led the entire mine to be closed, putting its workers out of a job for the time being.

New Artifact Sets

Vermillion Hereafter

Two-piece bonus: Increases Attack by 18% Four-piece bonus: After using an Elemental burst, the character will gain the Nascent Light effect which increases his Attack by 8% for 16 seconds. If the character loses HP, this bonus will increase by 10% stacking up to four times. It should be noted that the effect will stop once the character leaves the field or if an Elemental Burst is used again.

Echoes of an Offering

Two-piece bonus: Increases Attack by 18% Four-piece bonus: When Normal Attacks hit opponents, there is a 36% chance that it will trigger an effect called Valley Rite which increases Normal Attack damage by 60% of the users Attack stats. If a Normal Attack fails to trigger this effect, the odds will increase by an additional 20% for the next Attack.
